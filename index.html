The 2007 Season

This year's Virginia General Assembly session ran from January 10 through February 24. 1,458 bills were passed, of which Governor Kaine vetoed seven and signed 933 into law (the remaining 518 don't require his signature). All 140 seats in the House and the Senate are up for reelection this November, and then the bodies will reconvene in January 2008.
